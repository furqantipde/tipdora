# Monetization Strategy for Tipdora.fun

## 1. Google AdSense Optimization
Since the goal is high organic traffic, AdSense is the primary revenue stream.
**Recommended Placements:**
- **Header:** 728x90 Leaderboard (Desktop) / 320x100 Mobile Banner.
- **Sidebar:** 300x600 Sticky Skyscraper (High CTR).
- **In-Content:** Native Ads inside tool descriptions (e.g., between "How to Use" and "Formula").
- **Footer:** 300x250 Rectangle just above the footer links.
- **Auto Ads:** Enable Google Auto Ads with "Vignette" (interstitial) ads between page loads (very high revenue for calculator sites).

## 2. Affiliate Marketing
Integrate relevant affiliate links:
- **Finance Tools:** Link to Bank Loan Applications, Credit Cards, or Investment Apps (e.g., Robinhood, Coinbase) on EMI/Crypto pages.
- **Health Tools:** Link to Fitness Gear, Supplements, or Weight Loss Programs on BMI/Age pages.
- **VPN:** "Secure your crypto trading" links on Crypto Calculator.
