Understanding BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple estimate of body fat based on height and weight. It is widely used as a general indicator of whether a person has a healthy body weight.

BMI Categories

Category BMI Range
Underweight < 18.5
Normal Weight 18.5 – 24.9
Overweight 25 – 29.9
Obesity 30 or greater
