Age Calculator

How It Works

The Age Calculator determines the time interval between the date of birth and the current date. It calculates your age in completed years, months, and days.

Did You Know?

In most Western cultures, a person's age is the number of full years they have lived. However, in some cultures (like traditional East Asian age reckoning), people are born at age "1" and the age increases at the New Year.
