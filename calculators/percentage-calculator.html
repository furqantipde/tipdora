Percentage Formula Guide

Percentage means "per 100". It is a number or ratio expressed as a fraction of 100.

Basic Formula

(Value / Total Value) Ã— 100 = Percentage

Real World Examples

  • Sales Tax: If tax is 10% and you buy something for $50, you pay $5 in tax ($50 * 0.10).
  • Discounts: 25% off $100 means you save $25.
  • Tips: Leaving a 20% tip on a $40 meal is $8.
